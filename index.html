About Me

Passionate about quality and data-driven excellence

I am a Quality Assurance Specialist with over 5 years of experience in auditing, coaching, and process improvement across top companies like Khazna and Uber. My expertise lies in transforming complex data into clear, actionable insights that drive operational excellence.

Throughout my career, I've successfully implemented quality frameworks, designed comprehensive dashboards, and developed training materials that have significantly improved team performance. My approach combines Lean Six Sigma methodologies with hands-on data analysis to identify opportunities for enhancement and ensure consistent service quality.

At Khazna, I expanded my scope beyond traditional QA to audit second-line teams, create innovative quality frameworks for CSAT/DSAT analysis, and revamp customer-facing systems. My work has directly contributed to reducing inactive users, improving documentation processes, and maintaining high-quality standards across multiple communication channels.

I'm continuously seeking new challenges and opportunities to apply my analytical mindset and quality-focused approach to drive meaningful business improvements.
